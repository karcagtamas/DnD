# Iceshield Orcs

> An orc tribe displaced by the natural disasters caused by the elemental cults launches a series of raids against homesteaders in the eastern part of the valley. This adventure is designed for 4th-level characters.

- Orc raids are common occurences theroughhout the frontier
  - Small bands of orcs steal livestock or supply wagons
- The orcs from east of **Sumber Hills** became bolder
- The iceshield orcs are attacking farms and cattle ranches
  - Less than a day's ride from **Yartar**
- The farmers and ranches from region take steps to protect themeselves

## Background

- Around the wstern edge of the **High Forest**
- They become prominent threats
- They seek supply, and the homesteads are potentionel targets
  - They take any wagons, carts and lifestocks
  - They take the families as slaves and force the to tow
- Some indenpendent orc squads do not take priosoners
  - They kill the owners to occupy the land

## Setup

- Characters discover the burning homestea of the _Anderil Farm_
- Orcs attackecd, and took a few priosoners
- From there, the characters travel to the _Dellmon Ranch_, where they aid the defense until help from **High Forest** elves arrives

## Running the Siege

- The orcs easily defeat twice their number of commoners, but the defensive lines can improve the odds
- The ranchers need to survive

### Initial Surge

- The first sign: scouts appear
- Small bands of orcs spread out, surrounding the buildings
- With the blast of a horn, they all rush the structures simultaneously
- Party:
  - 40 _orcs_
  - 1 lone _orog_
    - And his second-in-command: orc Eye of Gruumsh
- When the orcs are in range, they throw javeling at any visible targets
- In the next round, they attack with meele weapons
- After 2 or 3 rounds, the orcs retreat
- The orcs repeat this for a several times and after, they start a different tactic

### Smoke Screen

- Late in the day, they orcs start a grass fire upwind from the compound to create cover themselves
- The area becomes lightlt obscured
- After 30 feet, the view is heavily obscured
- The orcs uses the smoke to move closer before launching

### Starting a Fire

- During the night, the orcs sneak close enough to the buildings to set them on fire
- They hope the defenders are unable to put the fires out and protected perimeter from breaches

### Relief Arrives

- At dawn, the orcs make a final push
- It should seem like they might overwhem the defenders in several spots and get inside the compuund
- Suddenly the melodious tone of an elvern war horn sounds
  - A company of tweint-five elven soldiers (mix of guard, acolytes, scouts and few veterans) arrives to help

## Conclusion

- The orcs are slain, scattered
- Farmers and ranchers hail the characters as heroes and pool meager resources to offer a reward of 25 gp per character
- Emerald Enclave and Order of the Gauntlet receive appropriate kudos
