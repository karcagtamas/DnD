# New Management

> When the innkeeper of the Bargewright Inn is called away, the characters are asked to step in and manage things for a few days. Zhentarim infighting makes what should be a routine task far more challenging. This adventure is designed for 3rd-level characters, although it is equally playable at lower and higher levels.

- Bargewritght Inn
  - Depends on the regular supply runs downriver by barge from **Yartar**
  - Inkeeper: **Nalaskur Thaelond**, Zhentarim operative
- The inkeeper wants estabilish a supply run to **Waterdeep**
  - He needs to go there, an he needs somebody to take care of the inn
  - He preferes a Zhentarim agent
- **Chalaska Muruin**
  - **Nalaskur** works with her, but he does not trust her
  - Cold-eyed human _veteran_
  - Senior Sword (master of the inn's forty guards)
  - She does not like **Nalaskur** (she want him to be removed)
- **Nalaskur** and **Chalaska** are spying on eachother
- **Nalaskur** does not want to appoint **Chalaska** as inkeeper (fustrate her)
- Unknows to **Chalaska** and **Nal`askur** an another Zhentarim agent established a cover identity as a handyman
  - Male dwarf spy: **Inglor Brathren**
  - He thinks that **Nalaskur** fill his pockets and **Chalaska** turns a blind eye to it
    - He fears **Chalaska** and don't want to accuse them without proof
  - He decides both need replacing

## Setup

- Encounter **Nalaskur**
- For tenday, promising pay and a good recommendation to his superiors
- He choose one character as an innkeeper
  - He telle where the keys are and the importance of those keys (_Key Duty_)
- He is in hurry and he needs to leave
- When the team refuse
  - **Nalaskur** leaves a note at the inn about the accepted job
  - The people from the inn go to the characters when the things go wrong
- The inn is full of Zhentarim interested people (scouts, spies and thugs)
- When no **Zhentarim** in the team
  - A befriended NPC asks and begs them
- Any number of _scouts_, _spies_ and _thugs_ might be in the inn (or on mission) - **Zhentarim** interests

## Key Duty

- All the keys are hung in the cabinet in the innkeeper's office
  - The cabinet is locked when no one is using the office
  - Keys are for the gates and doors
- The keys must remain safe from the foes of the faction
- The trades, negotations and business must remain secure
- The keys like a big human hand and red dots are painted on them
  - The red dots identify the locks
- Six keys are gone missing after **Nalaskur** left
  - **Inglor** took them and stashed them in a chest in the attic of the southern tower
- At the least the characters notices the missing keys
  - **Chalaska** asks for some sos she can lock up for the night
  - The keys opens the outer wall's main gate, two similar inner wall gates, meeting rooms in the southern inn tower, strong room at the base of the northern tower
    - The last room is where the factions keeps the stolen items, valuables and important objkect

### Search for the Keys

- When the situation is clear to **Chalaska**, she sounds alarm and doubles the guards, commands the search
- The player with the highest Insight who was in the taprool before the keys went missing
  - He remembers a well-dressed human male who left the tavern quicky right before **Chalaska** ask for the keys
  - DC 10 Insight
    - The men looked worried and held a small note in his hand
- **Chalaska** become suspicios about the new innkeeper, and orders to search the players
  - They disarm the players if they use violence (not kill)

### Unlucky Kendrin

  - He not know Kendring and afraid of **Chalaska** a little bit but he is honest

## Strong Room Burglary

- **Inglor** breaks into the Strong room and takes 500gp while the team is dealing with **Kendrin**
  - He also ransacks the room
  - He leave two distinctive silver fox-head buttons
    - Stolen from **Chalaska** and everyone recognise them
  - He leave the door ajar
- **Inglor** stashes 200gp into the chest (where the keys were)
  - Then he sneaks the keys and 300gp into **Chalaska**' bedroom
- He is not suspicious (because he is the handyman)
  - But he can be caught in action

## General Alarm

- After the burglary he goes the other side of the inn and spread words like:
  - Something odd is happening
  - Chalaska is responsible for the security
- Of the bulglary is not found, **Inglor** will find the ajared door
- He openly attack **Chalaska** behind his back

## Conclusion

- All clues point at **Chalaska**
- She bar herself into the Strong room with some loyal Swords
- An all search can discover the chest in the attic
- There are foorprint in the dusk (not **Chalaska**'s)
- **Nalaskur** returns
  - In the situtation that the players made
  - He discovers that 500gp is missing
  - A final search leads to **Inglor** (who is the only other staff)
    - If not, **Chalaska** is discredited and removed
      - **Nalaskur** loses face with the **Zhentarim** as do the players
