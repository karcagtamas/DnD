# Temple of Black Earth

- Northeast quarter of the dwarven ruins
- It was the main entrance of **Tyar-Besil**'s
  - Well fortified gate complex with barracks, guardrooms, armories and stables
  - It could stop the one or more recless attacks of orcs, trolls or giants
- Currently it is a ruin
  - Not by siege weapons

- Can be reached from the Ancient Stair
- It is beneath the Sacred Stone Monastery
