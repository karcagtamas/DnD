# Air, Earth, Fire, and Water

- Greate dungeons below the **Sumber Hills**

## Urgent Summons

- Urgent messages from the factions about one of the side treks (chapter 6)

## The Spy's Letter

- **Justran Deaehl**, the cellarer (ale buyer) of the **Helm at Highsun** is a spy of the **Cult of the Crushing Wave**
  - He reports about the characters (arrival, presence in the region)
  - He does not sign the letter, but if the characters find the reports in the **Temple of the Curshing Wave**, they can figure it out
    - Whose often work with **Justran** or **Helm At Highsun**, can recognize the scripts
      - **Kaylessa**, **Marlandro Gaelkur** and **Aerego Bethendur**
    - If the characters show the letter to everyone, **Justran** flee from the town
  - When confronted
    - He denies (clear forgery against him)
    - If the encounter is in the _Helm_ and the charges are weak, 5 _commoners_ joins against the players

### Poisoned Ale

- He might take out the characters
- **Justran** serve poisoned ale to them (_potion of poison_) - with adavanteg saving throw
  - The poison is weak (not lethal)
- Investigation reveals the source of the poison

### Development

- If the characters capture **Justran**, he revals that he is a **Cult of the Crushing Wave** member (after short interogation)
- Underground stream at **Rivergard Keep** lead to the temple below (he has never been at the temple)
- He knows that **Jolliver Grimjaw** and his followers are water cultists too

## The Temple of Elemental Evil

- Ancient dwarven citaled of **Tyar-Besil** beneath **Sumber Hills**
- Each cult control one path to the underground and the quarter of the city

### Infiltrating the Temples

- Character who dress in cultist gear, might can infiltrate the temple
- Human members of the cult have good change to spot impostors but fast talking characters can gain access (prophet, prophet issue can gain advantage)
  - The intimidated cultists insist to lead the party to the leader (escort)

### Driving the Cults into Retreat

- The first prophet (encountered) is in the temple, after the death of the first, the others go into the lower dungeons
- The dungeon is defeated if the prophet is killed or the monsters are eliminated in the shrine
