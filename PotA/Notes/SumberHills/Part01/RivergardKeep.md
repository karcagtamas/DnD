# Rivergard Keep

- Small, strong castle at the **Dessarin River**
- One of the Haunted Keeps
- Occupied by **Jolliver Grimjaw** (mercenary lord) and his sellswords
- They protect the river trade route from bandits and monsters
- Secret stronghold of the **Cult of the Crushing Wave**

## Investigating Rivergard

- **Grimjaw** and his gang work with smugglers to move stolen goods on the river
- They attack riverboats that refuse to pay protection money
- They actively recruit thugs and monsters to join the cult

> Egy kis kastély a hatalmas Dessarin folyóra néz, amely ezen a ponton jó fél mérföld széles. Egy kapuház és állj egy alacsony blöff tetején, de a falak a víz szélére épültek. A folyóparton egy másik alacsony torony őrzi a csónak medencéjét, ahol a keelcsónakok ki vannak kötve.
> A vár réginek és kopottnak tűnik, de az új faanyagok és zsindelyek azt sugallják, hogy a javítás folyamatban van. Egy fehér transzparens kék kesztyűvel repül a var felett.
> A bejárati kapu zárva van.

### Peaceful Approach

- Open and polite approach with plausable reason
  - The character who uses important inside knowledge (asking to see **Gar Shatterkeel**, or **Reash**, or to sign on with **Grimjaw**) is considered as important guest or dangerous spy
- If the players do not use knowledge and are rude or prove no reasonable excuse for entrance
  - They will be turned away
- 4 **bandits** escort the characters who enter peacefully from **K1**
- 3 **Crushing Wave reavers** escort the characters from **K11**
- The escorts take the team to the great hall **K16**
  - **Grimjaw** interviews the players

### Aggresive Approach

- **Grimjaw** and his men are not expecting an attack
- If they sneak in
  - They challanged by anyone who meet and they can speak out themselves from the situation

## Keep Features

- The cultists are busy repairing **Rivergard**
- Parts of the complex remain inaccessible (upper floors of the north tower and the water tower are filled with debris)
- Arrow slits: 9 feet above the ground level, 8 inches wide and 4 feet high
- Ceilings: 12 feet
- Defenders
  - **Jolliver Grimjaw** (Jolly)
  - The bandits who follows him for money, not for ideology
  - Not all the followers are loyal to **Grimjaw** or the cult
- Doors: _DC 10 Strength_ check
  - Exterior doors are reinforced: _DC 20 Strength_ check
- Light:
  - During day: Bright light in the interior spaces through windows and arrow slits
  - During night: Interior spaces are brighly lit with small oil lamps
- Locks: Thieves' tools and _DC 15 Dexterity_ check
- Treasure: Each warrior (2d10-2 gp in mixed coinage)
- Walls: 15 feet above outside the ground level, 10 feet above inside the ground level
  - 1 **bandit** on each of the four wall sections waling
