# Feathergale Spire

- In the **Sighing Valley**
- Haunted Keep
- Inhabitated Feathergale Society
- Aerial-mount enthusiasts who have peretensions of nobility
- They call themeselves Feathergale Knights
- Secretly the knights are dedicated to **Yan-C-Bin**
- They serve the cult as lookouts, messengers, spies and first line of defense
- They captured the ruins when the cult estabilished their cult in the old dwarven ruins beneath the **Sumber Hills**

## Spire Approach

- Unique environment
  - During day: azure sky encompasses the tower and gusty winds buffet it from all sides
    - The wind sighs through tick formations in the canyon
  - During night: the wind calms to a breeze and a light fog settles over the canyon
    - The spire then appears to float among the stars on a sea of clouds

> A Feathergale Spire egy sziklaoszlopból emelkedik ki a magasba, mérföldeken keresztül a legmagasabb pont. A fehér mészkőből épült és márvánnyal díszített torony egy csillogó kardra hasonlít, amely áthatol az égen.
> A kapuház a szemközti sziklára néz, felvonóhídja az egyetlen látszólagos belépési pont. Magas, széles ablakok veszik körül a torony alsó szintjét, csak a kapuház felőli oldalon hiányzik.
> A torony alapját nyitott bódék kör veszi körül, ahol találkozik a sziklával. Mindegyik bódé felett egy repülõ víziló szobra ugrik ki a torony aljából.
> A keleti Feathergale-torony alatt széles, viharos kanyon ásít a dombok között.

- Trail leads to a a drawbridge over Feathergale Moat and to the keep's main entrance (**S2**)
- Each level 20 feet
- The first level (**S1**) 400 fet above the ground
- In daylight the Pinnacle (**S11**) has a good vantage point and sees the approaching characters (a knight)
- A knight and two initiates at the enter hall
- The openly arriving party is welcomed, the sneaking or attacking party not (defense)

## Spire Features

- Ceilings: 18 feet high
- Defenders: (4 **Feathergale Knights** and their giant vulture are away - see **Knight's Quest**)
- Doors: Interior doors are made of wood, banded iron (most not have locke, but can be barred - DC 20 Strength)
- Floors: Made of whitestone
- Light: Many windows, the spire is bright at day and dim lit at night, if the reinforced shutters are closed the day becomes dim lit and dark at night
- Locks: Thieves tools and DC 15 Dexterity
- Treasure: Each knight has 2d10gp in mixed coinage and minor valuables at their locked chests in their cells
- Windows
  - Oversized windows on the ground level: 3 feet from ground, 6 feett tall and 8 feet wide
  - Smaller windows: Half of the oversized windows
    - Can beShutters reinforced with iron bands permit the windows to be shut and barred (like a barred door)
