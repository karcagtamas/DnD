# Cult Reprisals

- The prophets gain intel about the destroyed, eliminated **Haunted Keeps**
  - Facing them through dreams, visions and portents
  - The **Elder Elemental Eye** warns the prophets of danger and impels them to respond
- Four reprisals
  - Tremors
  - Skyriders
  - Reaver Ambush
  - Fiery Fangs
- Use reprisals
  - When Dangerous Information adventure hook is used and the characters trave to the attack site (see Reaver Ambush)
  - Choose one after the characters visit the first locale (**Beliard**, **Dessaring Road**, **Shallow Graves**, **Summit Hall**, **Wormford Rats**)
    - Reaver Ambush and Skyriders are good
  - Choose a second after the characters visit the first of the Haunted Keeps
    - Reaver Ambush and Skyriders are good (the opposite of the first)
  - Choose a third after the characters visit the third of the Haunted Keeps

## Tremors

- While the characters explore **Sumber Hills**
  - First hand intel about strange phenomena and dangerous monsters plaguing the area
- Use this any thime when the characters are travelling between town or search the hills for sites of interest

> A kopár vidéken toporogsz, amikor halk, távoli morajlást hallasz. Egy pillanattal később a talaj remegni kezd a lábad alatt. A remegés elég erős ahhoz, hogy kis kavicsokat indítson el a domboldalakról, és a cserjék és a cserjék egyik oldalról a másikra hullámzanak, de aztán alábbhagy. Egy pillanattal később borzalmas, rovarszerű lények, akkora, mint a lovak, elkezdenek kimászni a földből!

- Two _ankhegs_

## Skyriders

- The noisy adventurer questions gain attention of the **Howling Hatred** cult
  - They send a group of aerial raiders to eliminate or scare the characters

> Valahonnan fentről furcsa, károgó kiáltás ragadja meg a figyelmét. Hatalmas, szárnyas alakok hármasát veszed észre, amelyek feléd suhannak – óriási keselyűket! Minden madár a hátán hord egy harcost tompa kék páncélban, piszkosfehér köpennyel.

- Attackers
  - One **Feathergale knight**, two **Howling Hatred initiates** each mounted on **giant vulture**
  - They prefer to keep their distance and make ranged attacks
  - If the vulture is below half HP, the rider disengaged and retreats
  - If two rider is killed or retreated, the third follows them

### Skyriders - Treasure

- One of them has a map tucked into his boot (no treasures)
- The map about the valley (hand drawn) and a location marked with name "the Spire" and a strange arrow-like symbol by the site (air cult symbol)

## Reaver Ambush

- Few miles from **Bargewright Inn**
- Caravan ambush

> Egy kicsi, gondosan megdöntött főzőtűz parázslik ennek a véletlenszerű tábornak a közepén. Haifa tucatnyi ütött-kopott régi sátra és élelmes ládák hevernek szét, valamint állványok, amelyeken füstölt haldarabok száradnak. Számos heves tekintetű emberharcos ácsorog a sátra előtt, vigyázva a felszerelésére vagy csendesen beszélgetnek.

- The camp is occupied by one **Crushing Wave priest**, two **Crushing Wave reavers** and five **bandits**
- They attack strangers and attack them
- If all the **Crushing Wave** members are dead, the bandits flee or surrender

### Reaver Ambush - Development

- The captured **Crushing Wave** members do not talk, but the bandits talk
- The tell that they are mercanaries at the **Rivergard Keep** and work for **Jolliver Grimjaw**
- They can tell the location

## Fiery Fangs

- They send a pack of 3 **hell hounds** after the party
- The hounds follows scents
- Possible encounters
  - On travelling
    - The hounds are coming in a short distance
  - In an inn
    - The players wake up on screaming and tremors
    - Window breaks and shoutings
- The hounds fight until one of the is killed and the second is under half HP
  - They have collar with a mysterious bowl-like symbol (fire cult symbol)
  - **Thorsk Thelorn** in **Red Larch** has intel
    - From a crossing merchant about a mysterious gathering of druids in the **Sumber Hills**
    - He can prove directions to the **Scarlet Moon Hall**
