## Manticore

- Knights join the characters on the hunt

> Négy Feathergale Knight gyűlik össze a csúcson, mindegyik egy óriási keselyűn. A Hold megvilágítja a ködös kanyont a Feathergale Spire alatt. A messzi távolban a sötétség egyetlen szilánkja mozog, ki-be száll a ködből. Aztán az árnyék lesüllyed a felhők partja alá, és ismét eltűnik a szem elől.

- The hunt is in the sky

### Visibility

- The vision is limited 100 ft (because of the fog and night sky)
- The mounts know the spire and cliffs (avoiding)

### Hunting the Manticore

- The knights go together as a group
- The players can join, or create own group or more seperated groups (100 ft distance creates a group)
- Finding the Manticore
  - At the and of each minute spent searching: roll d20 (each group) + 1 (for each group member)
    - When 18 or higher: 100 feet of the manticore
    - Any group who find it, can engage
  - If the knight find the manticore, they blow their horns
    - In 1d6 rounds reach
- Battling the Manticore
  - The manticore finds until it has no targets to attack
  - Using its action: 3 Tail Spike attacks
  - If no players: each round one knight is injured and 8 HP reducing
  - When no enemy, the manticore flight away and the battle end
  - The manticore has 24 tail spikes
    - When no more tail spikes, it uses its turn to fly away
    - At the end each of these turns: all knights and players roll for DC 13 Perception
      - If all fail: the manticore is escaped
- Manticore Lost
  - After 10 minutes and all the group search was unsuccessful, the manticore returns to the lair
