# Scarlet Moon Hall

- **Elizar Dryflagon**
  - Fire cultist (one was a druid)
  - Circle of the Scarlet Moon knows the Rite of the Wicker Giant (ritual that can restore the nature balance)
  - Several group set up camps below the Scarlet Moon Hall, waiting for **Elizar**
  - The atmosphere is one of festive
  - The real druids eager to meet with **Elizar**

## Area Features

- The build was once a central tower with outer wall. Now the walls are ruined and the tower is under repair.
- Haze: Smoky haze that renders the area lightly obscured. A bright spot in the haze indicates where the sun or moon is
- Defenders: Fire cultists pretend to be servants of the **Circle of the Scarlet Moon**. Only **Elizar** has druid capabilities
- Fires:
  - Each bonfire deals 1d10 fire damage when enters or starts its turn there
- Slope:
  - Within the wall, within about 20 feet of each campire and the trails are walkable, the other terrais are difficult
- Tents:
  - Enough for a humand to crouch in
  - Bedrolls and clothing are scattered on the floors
  - Most have lantern
- Treasure: Visitors carry 2d10 gp in mixed coinage and minor valuables

## Ascending the Slope

> A dombot beborító párán keresztül egy romos falakkal körülvett, négyzet alakú torony látható. A torony előtt egy tüzes óriás alakú máglya ég. A torony alatti lejtőn hét máglya szétszórva, körülöttük sátrak csoportosulnak. Humanoid alakok húzódnak meg ezeknél a közelebbi tüzeknél. Egy ösvény vezet fel ezekhez a domboldali táborokhoz.

- Due to the haze, character can sneak pas ot overcome a camp without arousing the attention of the other
- Bear (Hc2), werewolves (Hc3) and worgs (Hc7) have keen senses (not depends on sight)
- The denziens of a camp cry out an alarm only if they clearly will lose
  - The reinforcement come in waves
- The characters can avoid the camps on the north side
  - Steep but navigable - without ability check

## Hc1 - Druid Camp

> Négy köpenyes ember – két férfi és két nő – kántál, miközben egy máglya körül állnak. A tűzfény peremén két hatalmas jávorszarvas rágcsál nyugodtan.

- The **druids**: Varigo, Mahoon, Iniri and Fariya
  - They looking forward to the **Rite of the Wicket Giant** and hope it leads to greater coop between the druids in the valley
  - They celebrating in hopes of a successful rite
  - They offer the characters food and drink (including a few sips from a small cask of brandy)
  - They try to encourage the group to stay for the night and witness the rite (occur tommorrow)
- The cult thinks they are unfit for the cult and **Elizar** plans to feed the wicker giant with them
- They fight only if attacked
  - They beg for mercy if half of them are down (including the elks)
- Reinforcements: Hc2 arrives in first wave (if disturbance is detected), Hc3 arrives if the disturbance persists

### Hc1 - Treasure

- Scroll of elemental bane and scroll of warding wind in a wooden case in one of their tents

## Hc2 - Cult Camp

> Két köntösbe öltözött ember áll a máglya előtt. A másik oldalon, egy földbe szúrt karóhoz kötözve, egy barna medve fekszik.

- 2 **Eternal Flame priests** (Aylbrith and Ingulf) - posing as druids of the **Circle of the Scarlet Moon**
- They have captured a **brown bear** to improve their disguise
- They warmly greet visitors who arent obviously hostiles
  - They try to find their reason for visiting the **Scarlet Moon Hall**
  - If the cultists do not get good answers, they attack
    - If the bear can't reach anybody he try to unleash: DC20 Strength
    - He attacks thee player who can reach or a cultist if not
- Reinforcements: Hc1 first (they try to talk rather than fight), If the disturbance is large or extended, Hc6 arrives

## Hc3 - Werewolf Camp

> Két zord ember ül egy nagy máglya közelében. Ritkán öltözöttek és nagyon szőrösek.

- Storol and Wiglaf, **werewolves**
  - They want to know what the druids up to, and the believe in the rite
  - They passed the cultists tests when they visited the camp, but they dont know their lychantropy
  - Any hostility causes them to turn into hybrid form and attack
- Reinforcements: Hc4 first, then Hc6 (they attack the characters and the werewolves too)

## Hc4 - Cult Camp

> Egy félig elf nő itt köntöst visel, míg három társa – egy ember nő és két ember férfi – masszív ruhát és mellvértet visel. A három páncélos kebabot süt a máglyájánál.

- An **Eternal Flame priest** and 3 **Eternal Flame guardians**
  - The priest (half-elf Lytin) looks like a druid and tries to do all the talking
  - Her guardians: Biart, Orm and Malin look like rangers
    - They are more nervous the longer any conversation lasts
- Reinforcements: Hc3 arrives first, but Hc5 might respond

## Hc5 - Fiddler's Camp

> Ez a máglya egy kicsit kisebb, mint a többi. Egy köntösbe öltözött tünde nő hegedül, miközben két szellem táncol a tűz feletti füst körül.

- This camp has real **druid**: Gariena, and 2 **sprites**: Flish and Afid
- They here to contribute the rite
- She has a sens of anticipation, but she wonders why she bever heard of this rite
  - She also wonders about the long preparations
- She fights if she is attacked, and happily shares the campfire with friendly characters, but no one can camp here
- Reinforcements: Hc4 arrives, they attack the players first, then Gariena (they think she is not good to be a cultist)

### Hc5 - Treasure

- Gariena has a coinpurse with 40sp, 18gp
  - She wars a silver ring (25gp) and carries a scroll of lesser restoration and a scroll of skywrite
  - If the characters gain her favor, she gives the scrolls to the thim to fight against the Elemental Evil

## Hc6 - Druid Camp

> Hárman szundikálnak a máglya közelében, miközben a negyedik egy botra támaszkodik, láthatóan iszonyúan unatkozik.

- Three sleeping **druids**: Comnall, Murcadh, Ragnad
  - Wake if the characters approach openly
- The fourth a **Crushing Wave priest** (Sauruki), sent by the water cult to figure out what the fire cult is up to here
- They are suspicious about the strangers, but Sauruki is curious who are they
- The cultists do not decied about this group, Sauruki knows the cultists are searching for recruits
- If the characters seem likely to distrupt the ritual, the druids attack
  - Sauruki tries to hang back
  - He showing the sign of the water cult, crossing his forefinger and touching his thumbs (hoping that the characters are connected to the water cult)
- Reinforcements: Hc7 arrives, Then Hc3

## Hc7 - Bugbear Camp

> Ebben a peremtáborban három testes alak áll a tűz közelében, köntösük csuklyáját felhúzva. Két worg pihen a tűzfény szélén.

- The hooded figures are 3 **bugbear**, accompained by 2 **worgs**
  - Previously some druids live here, but they were good for the cult and they were eaten by the wicker giant
  - They try to seem to druids from a distance, until the other camps are cleared out
- Reinforcements: Hc6 arrives first, they are not sure who to attack (the characters can sway them with a DC Persusasion)

### Hc7 - Treasure

## H1 - Courtyard

> A torony központi tornyát körülvevő falak romjain túl egy sátrakkal teli udvar található. Egy kétemeletes, humanoid alakú, fonott óriás ég itt fényesen. Két, köntösbe öltözött alak áll a fonott óriás közelében, fejüket imádkozva lehajtva.
> A torony javítás alatt áll, a keleti és nyugati oldalon körülbelül tíz méter magas faállványzat húzódik. Lámpások lógnak a faszerkezethez rögzített gerendákról és rudakról. Két számszeríjas őr figyeli az udvart az állványzatról nyugatra. A torony déli oldalán egy széles ajtót, amely valószínűleg egykor a főbejárat volt, romok torlaszolnak el.

- 2 **Eternal Flame priests**
  - Disguised as druids
  - Nobody should be in the courtyard, so they attack
  - One priests action is to stand before the wicker giant and cries: "Arise, and cleanse this place of the unworthy!"
  - He does this 3 times (if the priest dies, the other will continue)
- If the priests succeed to call the giant: H2
- Reinforcements:
  - The defenders in the kennel (H3) joins the battle
  - The guards react slowly on the scaffolding (H4) - they join on their second turn making ranged attacks
  - If the fight is long, the creatures from H5 join too
  - Camps also can come if the fight is long (start with Hc6 then Hc3)
  - Some campers might help to the characters

## H2 - Wicker Giant

> Hőhullámok áradnak a lángoló fonott óriásból, de a fa szerkezet magában tartja a lángokat anélkül, hogy azok felemésztenék.

- The wicker giant is a **fire elemental**
  - It remains until the scaffolding start to collapse, the priests call for help or the characters deal damage
- It attacks any creature what not look like fire cultist
- It burns anything nearby that's flammable
- On emerge the elemental burns the itself and the scaffolding

## H3 - Kennel

> Romos falak veszik körül ezt a sekély gödröt, ami valószínűleg egykor pince volt.

- 2 **hell hound** and 1 **Eternal Flame guardian**

## H4 - Scaffolding

- 30 feet high and equall long
  - Easy to climb and walkway about every 7 feet up
- 2 **Eternal Flame guardians**
  - Preffering to not go into melee battle
- The scaffolding can catch fire easily
- At start of each turn: 5 feet fire spreading to each direction
- In every minute during the fire: 1d2 10 foot sections collapse

## H5 - Upper Entry Chamber

- The door only reachable from the scaffolding
- The wall can be climbed because of the rough fieldstone (DC 10 Athletics)

> Ebben a melegen megvilágított szobában nagyméretű fa padló és kőlépcső vezet fel az északi falra.

- 2 **Eternal Flame guardians**

  - If the battle more than 3 rounds: H7 awaken and they warn **Elizar** in H8 and they have enough time to prepare for ambush

## H6 - The Downward Path

> Egy hatalmas törmelékhalom – a fenti szintről származó bútorok és padlóburkolat – áll a kamra közepén. Egy csoport apró tűzlény égeti a gerendákat az északi fal mentén, ahol egy alagút vezet lefelé.

- Rubble covers the old main entrance
- 5 **magmins** burn away the debris to make the tunnel in the northern floor larger
- They attack any one who is not fire cultist
- Tunnel
  - Goes downward and connects to E1 (**Temple of Eternal Flame**)

- Weak floor:
  - Like creaks loudly when steps on it
  - At the start of each turn: 1d8

      - It collapses: 15 feet to H5, 35 feet to H6

## H8 - Elizar's Chamber

> A szobában található egy hálózsák, egy íróasztal és egy könyvespolc. Az északi falon egy magas létra vezet fel a mennyezeten lévő csapóajtóhoz.

### H8 - Treasure

## H9 - Bats in the Attic

> Ez a kamra a torony ferde felső teteje alatt található. A mennyezeten lévő lyukak a szabadba vezetnek. Egy ilyen nyíláson keresztül egy rövid létra vezet nyugatra. A szobában erősen érződik a padlót borító ürülék szaga.

- 4 **giant bats**

  - 3 feet wide circular opening

  - 15 feet fown to H8

## Conclusion

- When the characters defeat **Elizar** and his followers, the scenario is over
- The tunnel from H6 leads to E1 in **Temple of Eternal Flame**
