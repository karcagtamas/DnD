# Sacred Stone Monastery

- Lich **Renwick Caradoon** made his home here
- Home of reclusive order of mysterious monks (earth cultists in disguise)
- Surface headquarters of the **Black Earth** cult and guards access to the unholy vaults of the **Temple of Black Earth** below

## Investigation the Monastery

- The monks keep their low profile
- They quitly recruit monsters and kidnap travelerrs to work as slaves

### Visistors Not Welcome

- The groups are not welcomed, they will be turned away (the storey does not matter)
  - "The abbess doesn't allow visitors to distrub our meditations"
  - They will be attacked, if the characters refuse to leave
- If the characters ask about the Mirabar delegation
  - "They aren't here" and they turn the party away
  - DC 10 Insight - sesnse monks are exchanging furtive glances

### Recruits or Members

- One option to enter if the characters persuade the monks that they want to join
  - Need to ask for **Hellenrae**, **Marlos Urnrayle**, **Miraj Vizann** (proof)
  - The characters can be escorted to **Qarbo** (M9)

### Disguises

- The monks normally wear hooded robes and gargoyle masks made of gilded tin that cover their faces
- The disguised characters can move in the monastery without being challanged (they need to speak not too much)
- The gargoyles in area M8 and M14 don't attack disguised characters

### Infiltration and Combat

- The undisguised will be identified as intruders and attacked
- They don't have organized defense
- After detect, they stand ground and fight (not raise alarm)
- The nerby rooms can hear the noise of fights

## Monastery Features

- The monastery is in a good shape (except for the eastern end of the building (M16, M17))
- Ceilings: 12 feet high
  - The mines have 6 feet hight ceilings
- Defenders: Mostly cultists, others are orogs or mercenaries
  - The lich **Renwick** (M16) is not with the cult
- Doors:
  - Made of wood
  - DC 10 Strength to force open a locked door
  - Exterior doors are reinforce with iron bands and usually locked
    - DC 15 Strength to force open
- Light:
  - During the day, the windows provide bright lights
  - At night the monastery and mines are dark
- Locks:
  - Thieves' tools and DC 15 Dexterity
- Treasure
  - The masks are worthless
  - The denizens may carry 2d10 gp in mixed coinage and minor valuables
  - The other monks does not have valuables
- Walls:
  - The roof of the monastery is 25 feet above the ground
  - The walls are easy to be climbed because of the patches
  - The wall around the garden (M14) is 10 feet high
- Windows:
  - Narrow and tall (like arrow slits)
  - Each 10 feet above the ground outside
