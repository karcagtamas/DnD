# Siege of Vallaki

- This is the events before the big ending
- Vallaki have sieged during the night
- The characters will get up at dawn in the Blue Water Inn
  - Urwin give information about the events and ask help from the team
  - The leader is shouting at the East Gate

## Status

- Kiril (the leader of the werewolves) has returned with an army
- They are surrounding the village
  - Werewolves, zombies, wolves, vampires, druids, blights
- The paths are blocked, no escape way

## Preparations

- Kiril is demanding Ireena for Strahd or he will destroy the village with the power of Strahd
- If the team refuses the give out of Ireena, they will start to breach the city to get her
- Kiril captured Ismark Kolyana (brother of Ireena), Emil and Zuleika

## Conflict

- The horde starts the siege
- They bring a huge walking tree (druids)

### Option 1
