## Lore

### Barovian Lore

- Devil Strahd
  - Can summon wolves and vermin
  - Can transform into a bat, a wolf or a cloud of mist
  - Humanoid form it can dominate you with its powerful gaze
  - Running water burns like acid
  - Sunlight burst into flame
  - Cant enter into a residence without invitation
- Beliefs and superstitions
  - Two divine force - Morninglord and Mother Night
  - Morninglord dominates before, but now he does not answer to the prayers
  - Mother Night presence is stronger, but she not answer too - the villagers think Strahd is the mother's curse
  - Vistani serve the devil Strahd, allowed to leave Barovia
  - Never harm raven, unfortune

### Vistani Lore

- Strahd
  - Royal bloodline
  - He died centuries ago, undead
  - Many consorts, but one true love: barovian peasent girl: Tatyana (not know what happened)
  - Strahd named his castle after his mother, the beloved Queen Ravenovia
  - Strangers not welcome in the castle without invitation
- Barovia
  - Strahd conquered this land centuries ago and named it after his father, King Barov
  - Strahd uses batsm wolves and other creature to spy
  - Barovians are simple, frightened people, but some have old souls, but many do not
  - The soulless know nothing and fear. Don’t cry, no charm, no hope, no spark
  - Krezk to west, Vallaki in heart of the valley, Barovia to east
  - Old windmill between Vallaki and Barovia. Avoid it
- Stick on the road: Wild druids, ghost, wolves and werewolves in the Svalich Woods
