# Cloack of the Niffin

- Wondrous Item, legendary

## Overview

This cloak is the culmination of high level magic users losing grip on an unimaginably complex and powerful spell. The might of the spell and the casters themselves turned into raw magical energy and coalesced into this form. This untamed and unthinking item only shows its potential while helping someone apt tapping into the wellspring of magic.

## Dormant State

- The wearer gains +1 to their Spell Save DC while wearing this cloak
- When the wearer uses the Flexible Casting Feature they can immediately make a melee spell attack, on a hit the target suffers d8 force damage equivalent times to the spell slot’s level that was gained or expended
- When the wearer would drop unconscious instead without dropping prone their innate magical energy takes over controlling their body. The wearer still has to make death saves, this ‘transformation’ lasts until the wearer is stabilized or dead. The wearer can still use their full turn to act as they were not unconscious in this state. Every spell cast in this state is considered to be under the Empowered Metamagic Feature. While in this state the wearer can only speak phrases needed to cast spells. You can’t use this feature again until you finish a long rest.

## Awakened State

- When the wearer drops unconscious the Cloak of the Niffin doesn’t get control over them.
- If Flexible Casting is used and the wearer reduces a creature to 0 HP in the turn when it is used, the wearer gains Sorcery Points equal to the felled creature’s Challenge Rating (CR). If no more Sorcery Points can be gained by this effect as a Free Action the would be gained Sorcery Points can be converted to a spell slot immediately instead.
