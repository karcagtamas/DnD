# Grave Warden

## Overview

> Weapon (greatsword), legendary
> According to legend this blade was manifested from the Divine Gate that keeps the realms of the mortals and immortals separate.

### Dormant State

- The wielder gains a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon.
- While wielded by a good aligned paladin the properties of Grave Gurdain change to the following: Versatile (2d6)
- Living creatures killed by this blade cannot be resurrected. Their names appear on the middle of the blade.

### Awakened State

- The bonus to attack and damage rolls increased to a +2.
- Grave Call. The wielder can make special ranged attack against a creature the wielder can see. The attack bonus for this attack is Grave Warden's attack bonus. The dealt damage consists only from this weapon's damage bonus and from any Smite's damaged. All of the dealt damage is necrotic damage.
